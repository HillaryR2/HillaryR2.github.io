Project #1:

Project Statement #1:

My topic is about social media negatively impacting teenagers in nowadays society. This topic is important to me because it’s a worldwide problem most teenagers experience. This ongoing issue can affect their mental health in many ways, such as: develop anxiety, body dysmorphia and depression, cause them to isolate themselves due to the feeling of loneliness or may embrace social media more than real life, etc. Some important information I would like people to know is that social media is a big factor of teenagers having low self-esteem due to unrealistic body images and looks they see on an everyday basis. For this collage, I chose all pictures that I found meaningful and are able to bring awareness to this.
